This is what we're all about.

S.M.A.L. is a full-service audio/video/photo production & event company. We act as a community, cooperative and experiential learning center for musicians and creatives located in Minneapolis-Saint Paul MN area.​

What We Do?

Studia Musique offers an Experiential Learning Program for musicians and aspiring audio engineers located in the Twin Cities metro area ages 13+ and up interested in learning Audio Production as well as servicing emerging, struggling and low-income musical artist's in and around the Twin Cites Metro Area.

We also offer Photography, Videography and Audio Production Services including headshots, concert photography, audio recording, mixing/mastering, music videos & more! It is our vision to creative a space for artist's to meet, connect, collaborate, create and grow together!

Artist Cooperative

Become a monthly supporting member and get 24-hour access to our space & equipment list for mixing mastering producing and rehearsals; became a part of our artist community and get connected to other artists here in the twin cities!

Affordable Recording Program:

The affordable recording program is a submission based scholarship program, designed to give local, low income, young and emerging musical artist's an opportunity to record an Album, EP or Single for free or a reduced/discounted rate; while working with both experienced & up and coming producers & engineers!

Experiential Learning "In-Studio Sessions"

The "In-Studio" Sessions offer a real world, hands on learning experience, working with professional producers/engineers and local artists inside our studio, learning how to operate both professional high-end equipment in sound treated environments and how to make your own gear work for you at home!

Events & Fundraising

Studia Musique belives in hosting community events and creating safe open space for artist's to meet, collaborate, create and grow! We also create fundraising events using music as a catalyst to support artists & small businesses in need! Some events we host include: Open Mics, Artist Mixers, Creative's Contests, Concerts/Booking, Fundraising and other Events.

Career Consultations

Studia Musique (S.M.A.L.) offers Strategic Planning Consultations to assist artist's in setting clear, attainable career goals set to help them meet their long term career goals. We also offer booking and other personalised career services for musicial & creative artists!

What Are The In-Studio Sessions?

The In-Studio Workshop Sessions offer a donation based, hands-on learning workshop opportunity aimed toward all ages learners, intertested in taking their DSLR Video/Audio Recording skills to the next level. We feature 2-4 local artists each month

Gallery Openings

We consign and buy local artist's work/photography to show and sell with a 25-45% commission/tax on all work sold in our gallery
